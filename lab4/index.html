Task 1: Everyday Task - Making a grilled cheese sandwhich

  1. Go to the kitchen
  2. Grab some bread, butter, cheese, and a pan
  3. Turn on the stove
  4. Put the pan on the fire to heat up the pan
  5. Add some butter to the pan
  6. Lower the temperture and add two bread to the butter
  7. Put cheese on one side of both breads
  8. Flip one bread to the other bread where both cheese sides are connecting
  9. Flip sandwhich
  10. Remove sandwhich from pan to a plate
  11. Eat the sandwhich
